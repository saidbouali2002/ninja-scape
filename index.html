About Game

Upon discovering that malevolent spirits are preparing to invade the living world, the ninja begins his adventure in the Black Forest surrounding an ancient temple—the portal to the realm of the dead. In this eerie world, he’s surrounded by angry spirits, mythical monsters, and deadly traps. His mission is to locate magical stones that hold great power. If he collects them all, he will have the chance to close the portal and prevent spirits from wreaking havoc on humanity.
  • Stealth and Combat: Players rely on stealth skills to avoid enemies when outnumbered and use advanced combat techniques for intense face-offs with opponents.
  • Puzzles and Challenges: The game offers numerous challenges that require players to solve puzzles, dodge traps, and find hidden paths leading to the powerful stones.
  • Upgrades and Special Abilities: Throughout the journey, the ninja gains new combat skills and upgrades that strengthen his abilities to take on stronger monsters.
